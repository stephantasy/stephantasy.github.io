Introduction

Who am I?
Stephantasy

Full time developper since 2009, I created Stephantasy Studio only a few years ago in 2016.

I became a developer in a second career, when I decided to do what I loved the most. Since then, I analyze, conceive, imagine, create, program as much in my work as during my free time!

I like discovering and learning new things. One day, I realized that I had many projects everywhere, sometimes not even finished yet. So I wanted to gather them all and create a kind of portfolio. This is how Stephantasy Studio was born.

Miscellaneous

  • Home Automation

    A monitoring and control system designed to monitor my home. It is build with Xiaomi Gateway and sensors, Raspberry Pi, Domoticz and more… (Still in development)

  • Monitoring system with remote control

    A monitoring and control system designed to monitor premises. When an intruder was detected, a video was recorded and an email was sent to the supervisor. At any time, the system could be controlled by a remote PC.

    VB6, LogMeIn

  • Fire detection system in TGV

    A fire detection system. This prototype was designed for TGVs. There was a control station with fire and smoke detectors in the locomotive, and a distant station to manage status and alarms. Data were sent by GSM.

    Siemens S7-200, Micro-Win, C++, Ladder, VB6
